## Copywriting Goldmine
### Ben Settle-style Bullets ที่รวบรวมได้:
1. "What if I told you the reason you're broke isn't because you don't make enough money — but because no one ever taught you how to keep it?"
2. "The 3 'traps' that keep 90% of salarymen poor... and how to escape them without eating nothing but instant noodles"
3. "Why 'pay yourself first' is the only financial advice you'll ever need (and how to set it up in 10 minutes)"
4. "Credit cards aren't the enemy. Your lack of a system is."
5. "The 50/30/20 rule is bullshit. Here's the version that actually works for Thai salarymen."
