**Source trace**: Objection #1 — "This sounds manipulative — I don't want to become like them." This takeaway directly frames the book as defensive, not offensive. It also appeals to the genuine prospect by excluding the people they HATE.

### Variation B: The "This Requires Work" Takeaway
"There is one thing you should know before you buy: this is not a magic pill. You will not read this book tonight and walk into work tomorrow as an untouchable political master. The techniques in here require observation, practice, and a willingness to stay calm when your instincts scream at you to react. But if you're willing to learn the game the way you learned your profession — systematically, over time, until it becomes instinct — then you're exactly who I wrote this for."

**Source trace**: Objections about "it's too complicated." Tone calibration says "Urgent problem + measured solution = credibility." This takeaway is honest about the effort required — which makes the promise MORE believable, not less.

"This book is for people who work in organizations with hierarchies — corporations, government agencies, hospitals, universities, mid-size companies.
