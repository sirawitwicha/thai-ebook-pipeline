**2. Influencing others**
Some power is derived from your position or your authority – you have
people who work for you, and you can ‘simply’ tell them what to do. But
the corporate world is changing, and many of us are now in flat or ‘matrix’
organisations, ones in which you need to influence people who are entirely
separate from your department and hierarchy. This requires a different,
more calculated approach to influencing, one which you will likely need to
practice. This chapter covers the do’s and don’ts of this process.
