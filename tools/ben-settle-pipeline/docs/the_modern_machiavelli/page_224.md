- The **decision-killer** is anyone who has the ability to block a deal
from happening, even if it has been approved. This usually
someone directly impacted by the outcome and who is on a
decision-making governance board or who has a close
relationship to the decision-maker themselves. These are most
obvious when a deal will lead to a loss of power/prestige/money
in a certain department, and you will need to find a way to keep
them happy to get it through. The usual approach is to provide
something nice to the decision-killer themselves, so they feel
better about the hit their group is about to take. Keep the various
currencies in mind to identify those which may work here.
