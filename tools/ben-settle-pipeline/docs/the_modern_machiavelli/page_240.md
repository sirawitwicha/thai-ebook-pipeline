**4. Risky situations**
Talent at office politics is a great thing to have, but sometimes you find
yourself in a situation which is *dangerous*. In other words, the outcome may
lead to you having major setbacks to your career, or even losing your job
entirely. In these situations you need to be sure that you are flexible, that
you at least try one last time to win, and that you cover your arse for the
inevitable moment when things go wrong.
