**7. Appendix: Further reading**
Education never ends, and there are no lack of books out there to get you further along the path towards mastering office politics. Here are a few which we have found to be particularly useful:
- **21 Dirty Tricks at Work**, Mike Phipps and Colin Gautrey
- **Crucial Conversations**, Patterson, Grenny, McMillan, Switzler
- **Enlightened Office Politics**, Michael Dobson, Deborah Dobson
- **Influence**, Robert Caldini
- **Influence without authority**, Allen Cohen, David Bradford
- **Never Split the Difference**, Chris Voss
- **Office Politics**, Oliver James
- **The Office Politics Handbook**, Jack Godwin
