All of this means it can be helpful to cover up your artwork
during working hours. This depends on what tattoo you have and where it
is, but long pants, long-sleeved shirts, and clever use of accessories such as
scarves or large watches can go a long way towards shielding it from view.
Alternatively you can simply show it to the world, because the world likely
doesn’t care – just check with HR first if any policies exist, and be ready for
some level of judgement.
