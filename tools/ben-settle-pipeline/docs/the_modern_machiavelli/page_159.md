**0. Dealing with subordinates**

Progression up the corporate ladder will *almost* inevitably bring you into a
management position. Although some careers can go far based on personal
responsibility alone, the majority will eventually cap out at a reasonable if
not amazing level. The subsequent steps will require you to direct multiple
efforts towards a single goal. In other words, you will need to *lead*.

Leading is a skill, and like all other skills it can be trained and developed.
Those who can lead and manage a team well are a major benefit for any
company, and this in turn leads to more importance for you. Which means
that if you are interested in developing your career, then you need to work
on your ability to lead and manage groups of people.

How do you manage this? The following sections will help you develop this
vital skill.
