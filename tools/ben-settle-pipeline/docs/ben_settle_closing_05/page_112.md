# Chapter 7
**Closing The Sale
Like A Champ**

“Please. I’ve sold sin to saints for centuries.
You think I can’t close one little deal?”

— Crowley
Supernatural

You got your headline, your opening paragraph, your story, and your
bullets... now it’s time for the 5th and last part of the Copy Slacker formula,
which is to close the deal and...

**Get Paid.**

This is where you’re going to layout the guarantee, if you have one.
You’re going to layout any bonuses, if you have them. And you’re going to ask
them to buy, and give the terms of your offer, and the deadline (always, always,
always include a deadline, more on this in a moment). I like to go into the close
right after the bullets. If you did the first 4 parts right, did your research right,
and are offering them something they want... then you’ve got them all dressed
up wanting to go somewhere.

That “somewhere” being, of course...

**To Buy What
You’re Selling.**

There are many ways you can do this.

But, I am a big fan of writing some kind of short, punchy connector
sentence or paragraph, and then making the offer. I’ll show you examples of
that when we analyze the ads in Appendix 1. But there are four components I
usually use to do it. Sometimes I use all of four. Sometimes I only use one of
them, it just depends on the situation and the offer.
