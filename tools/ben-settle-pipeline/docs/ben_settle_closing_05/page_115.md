**Offer To Buy Them**
**Your Competitor's Product!**

I did this many years ago for a martial arts school.

We gave people the first lesson free, and if they didn't think it was the
single best hour they ever spent learning self defense, he'd buy them a lesson
at any other self defense school of their choosing.

The result?

To my knowledge, not a single person ever took him up on it. But, it did
get people coming to his school in droves, and made him a lot more money as a
result.

And anyone can do this with a little imagination.

**"If this isn't the best book about real estate investing you've ever**
**read, let me know and I'll send your money back including your**
**shipping fees, you can keep the book, and I'll buy you a copy of my**
**closest competitor's book just for wasting your time!"**

That's as gutsy as the above guarantees, so make sure you have your
product-creation game tight. But that's the kind of drama that'll make your
sales copy stand out from everyone else's, get your offers a lot more attention,
and very likely get you a lot more sales, too.

**Close Component #2:**
**Bonuses**

I am a huge fan of using bonuses to bribe people into buying from my
offers. The more the merrier. In fact, there's an old school copywriting saying
about not selling the product as much as you sell the premium (i.e. the
bonuses).

But I love to pile on the bonuses.
