**Why It's All About**
**The Bullets, Chuckles...**

Bullet writing is the most important “writing” part of sales copy.

It's probably the #1 mechanical skill (as the great Ken McCarthy once described it) to learn, and it's not uncommon for the best copywriters to say they start with bullets, revolve everything around their bullets, and even write their copy in “bullet” form...

**Even If It That Copy**
**Isn't Made Up Of Bullets!**

That may sound like a paradox, but I'll get more into that in a bit.

For now, just realize it's all about the bullets.

Your headline is nothing more than a version of your best bullet. The more bullets you have, probably the more sales you'll make assuming you did your homework and know what the market wants, and have studied your product deeply enough.
