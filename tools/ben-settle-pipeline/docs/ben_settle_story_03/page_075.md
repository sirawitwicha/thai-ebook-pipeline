I'll give you a couple examples.

In Ken McCarthy's excellent book *The System Club Letters* he tells a story
about a stock trader who saw some weird anomaly in the stock market, that
helped make his firm $75 million in a single day. He was a hero after that. And
for the next two years he worked like crazy to try to repeat his success since
the commissions on such deals are astronomical. After months of frustration
not finding another such deal, one of his friends said, "hey remember that
thing that you made all that money from, isn't that happening over there in
Hong Kong right now?" And the guy did some research and, sure enough, it
was about to happen again. This time, with hindsight on his side, he was able
to negotiate a better deal with his bosses to get a bigger commission. And,
when all was said and done, he ended up making his firm $500 million.
