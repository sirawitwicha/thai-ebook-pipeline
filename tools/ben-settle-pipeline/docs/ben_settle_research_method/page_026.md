**13. What income bracket are they in?**

It's going to be an uphill battle selling a high ticket offer to somebody
who doesn't have any money. On the other hand, to someone who has money
cheapening your product and constantly talking about your low prices could be
a turnoff. High income people think completely differently than low income
people in many cases when it comes to prestige and status vs necessity and
baragains.

Obvious?

Maybe, but it's astonishing how much copy ignores this basic attribute.

**14. What are their political views?**

Are they Republican? Are they Democrat? Are they Socialists? Are they
Communists? Are they Libertarians?

Are they apolitical and not care?

Do they not even think about it? Or are they obsessed with it? Who are
they voting for? Who will they not vote for and why, and do they vote for the
lesser of two evils or do they vote strictly on principle or along party lines?

You may never use this in your advertising, but you should know this
about them, as it shapes their values, their buying decisions, and even who
they buy from or will refuse to buy from.
