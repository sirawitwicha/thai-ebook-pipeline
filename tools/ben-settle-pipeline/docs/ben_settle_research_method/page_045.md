You can also look at vintage ads nobody is using anymore. As I
mentioned before, comic book ads are perfect for this, and you will find a lot of
interesting appeals, ideas, benefits, and things you may not be thinking about
now. Every market changes and evolves. But some things are universal and
"evergreen." And that's why I like those old comic book ads, and other high-
selling direct response marketing ads. The longer an ad ran, the more
successful it was. And, if you're anything like me...
