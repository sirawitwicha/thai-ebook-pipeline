**How To Get These Answers?**

- **The first thing is to talk to people in your market.**

This is easier and more practical for some markets than others. If you
sell something in the health niche for people who have arthritis, you can talk to
people with arthritis, and get them to tell you stories about what their day is
like, what it feels like when they get up in the morning, how much of a burden
it is for them and their loved ones. In weight loss, I talked to overweight women
and found out the sadness they feel you never hear talked about in other
weight loss ads just nattering on about how much weight their prospects can
lose. You can read (and see it analyzed) my highest selling weight loss sales
letter later in this book. The story section touches on all kinds of pains and
insecurities I could only get by talking with (and being a fly on the wall of
certain forums) people in the market.

Same with any kind of market.

The more you can talk with people in that market, get them to tell you
stories (very important), and just let them talk, with you saying nothing at all
except “tell me more about that…”, the more raw data you'll get that can be
shaped into copy, and will answer the questions posed in this chapter.

- **Go where they hang out online.**

anything. Read the comments and passionate threads your market participates
