**18. Who wears the pants in the family when it comes to making buying
decisions?**

This is another vital question to ask. I don't know what the exact
percentage is, but the wife of the house often makes the buying decisions.
Sometimes the man does. Or maybe you're writing primarily to single men or

If your prospect has to get "permission" to buy, or even so much as seeks

**19. How old is your customer?**

Are they millennials, are they generation X, are they baby-boomers, are
they World War II generation? You're going to talk differently to each
generation, depending on their age, experiences, biases, beliefs, and
generational peculiarities.

**20. What gender is your customer?**

same ads.

Go for the primary buyers.
