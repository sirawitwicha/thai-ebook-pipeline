**More On Sales Letter Structures...**

Another thing I want to cover is sales letter structures.

This book is primarily about the 5-step Copy Slacker formula of
headline, opening paragraph, story, bullets, close. And this works especially
well selling informational products, and products to cold leads who don't know
who you are. If you observe a lot of high selling ads they all use this structure
— more or less — but I also don't want you to feel like you're trapped by it,
either.

By that I mean, not every sales letter will need all 5 parts.

I have written ads designed to look like reports, and had no bullets, just
a headline, opening sentence, story, and close. I've written ads that had no
headline, opening paragraph, bullets, and a close.
Sometimes you only have to write a headline, an opening paragraph, and the
