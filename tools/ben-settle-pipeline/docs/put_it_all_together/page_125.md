# Chapter 8
**Lashing It All Together**

**"Really, it's just a question of reassembling the components**
**in the correct sequence."**

**— Dr. Manhattan**
**Watchmen**

Even though there are still a lot of "moving parts" to deal with, I don't
know how to make it more simple than the 5-step Copy Slacker formula:

1.  Headline
2.  Opening paragraph
3.  Story
4.  Bullets
5.  Close

So now that you know what these five steps are and you've got your
research, you have the raw material and it's time to lash everything together
and starting building.

i.e. start the actual writing part.

And so, your next step is to take all that research you've done, and all
the notes you've taken during the research phase, and...

**Chaotically "Plug" Everything**
**Into The Formula!**

What that means is this:

Write the five steps on a blank sheet, and put at least a good page
between each step. Write "headline" in huge font at the top of the document.
Then do the same, spaced one page apart, with the other four steps. And then,
take your notes and start putting ideas, notes, etc that go with their
corresponding section.
