**How Much Do You Teach In An Ad?**

Sometimes you might be in a situation where you write an ad to a colder
audience, and you want to give more of an appearance of a report or training,
but it’s really just a disguised sales pitch.

You will see this in direct mail with magalogs, for example.

Or also, in space ads.

Online you can do a lot of this, too, and it can be very effective —
especially in VSL’s (video sales letters).

My advice for when you want more of a “disguised” pitch is to follow this
advice I once read as closely as possible:

**“Be Very Specific About The Problem**
**But Very Vague About The Solution”**
