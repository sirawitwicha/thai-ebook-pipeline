**How To Structure Sales Copy**
**For Maximum Believability.**

Remember, belief is everything.

One fully believed claim is worth 1,000 half believed claims. And the
great Gary Bencivenga ruled the copywriting world by focusing on proof and
believability over claims and hype. And what Parris taught was, exactly how to
build an ad so proof is “built-in” by doing this:

1.  Tell the story (i.e. a problem someone had and what happened)
2.  Then explain the “mechanism” (i.e. “our product has an extra
    molecule that…”)
3.  The hard data and statistics, etc (i.e. studies from major
    universities, etc)
