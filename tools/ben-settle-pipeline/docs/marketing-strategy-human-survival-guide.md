## 1. Product Line Architecture

### The Core Product (Hero Offer)
**Human Survival Guide V2** — Thai-language book, 15 กฎ (rules), 3 parts. 
- Part 1: เลิกเป็นเหยื่อ (Stop Being Prey) — Rules 1-5
- Part 2: เรดาร์สแกนคน (People Radar) — Rules 6-10
- Part 3: อาวุธเงียบ (Silent Weapons) — Rules 11-15
- **Estimated price**: ฿397-497 ($12-15) for ebook; ฿697-997 ($20-30) for print+digital bundle
- **Tone**: Aggressive, wake-up-call, "big brother who's been through it"

### Product #2 (BUNDLE PARTNER): The Office Politics Playbook (English)
From our `final-letter.md` — the 12 manipulation techniques, counter-tactics, and influence-building system.
- **Why this bundle works**: Same domain (workplace manipulation survival), DIFFERENT depth. Human Survival Guide teaches the MINDSET and BOUNDARIES. The Playbook teaches the specific TACTICS and PSYCHOLOGY behind each manipulation pattern. Complementary, not overlapping.
- **Bundle price**: Both books for ฿697 ($21) — save 30% vs buying separately
- **Positioning**: "You learned to protect yourself in Thai. Now learn the psychology behind the attacks — from the English research that the manipulators don't want translated."

### Product #3 (ORDER BUMP): ชุดสคริปต์เอาตัวรอด (Survival Scripts Pack)
5 fill-in-the-blank templates localized for Thai workplace contexts:
1. **CYA Documentation Script** — บันทึกการขโมยเครดิต (documenting credit theft)
2. **J.A.D.E. Refusal Script** — ปฏิเสธแบบไม่ต้องแก้ตัว (saying no without justifying)
3. **Grey Rock Response Phrases** — 20 ประโยคก้อนหิน (20 stone-wall responses)
4. **Meeting Confrontation Script** — สคริปต์เรียกคืนเครดิตในที่ประชุม (reclaiming credit in meetings)
5. **HR Reporting Script** — แจ้ง HR แบบป้องกันตัว (reporting to HR the RIGHT way)
- **Price**: ฿197 ($6) — impulse-buy territory
- **Why it converts**: The book teaches the WHAT and WHY. This gives them the exact WORDS to say. "You just read how J.A.D.E. works. Here are the exact sentences to type TOMORROW."

### Product #4 (UPSELL): 12 เทคนิครับมือคนเฮงซวย — Video Masterclass
Video training walking through each of the 12 manipulation techniques with role-play demonstrations in Thai workplace scenarios.
- 12 modules, 15-20 min each (~4 hours total)
- Each module: technique explained → real example → role-play demo → counter-tactic walkthrough
- **Price**: ฿1,497 ($45) — positioned as "workshop replacement" (live workshops cost ฿5,000+)
- **Modules**: Gaslighting, Credit Theft, Baiting, Projection, Love Bombing, Triangulation, Flying Monkeys, Quiet Firing, Scapegoating, The Smear Campaign, Strategic Incompetence, The Golden Boy/Girl Dynamic

### Product #5 (DOWNSELL): ออดิโอ้บุ๊ค + Field Guide Bundle
If they decline the masterclass, offer the lighter version:
- Audio book of Human Survival Guide (MP3, 3-4 hours)
- The 12 Techniques Field Guide (quick reference card)
- **Price**: ฿497 ($15)

### Product #6 (CROSS-SELL / BACKEND): The Office Politics Playbook — English Edition
Already positioned as the bundle partner. Also works as standalone purchase for bilingual professionals. Could also be translated to Thai as a separate product later.

## 4. Email Marketing Strategy

### Sequence 1: Pre-Purchase Welcome (5 emails, 7 days)

| Email | Day | Subject (Thai) | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Value Bomb** | 0 (instant) | "3 สัญญาณว่าคุณกำลังเป็น 'เหยื่อ' ในออฟฟิศ" | Deliver lead magnet. Establish voice. No pitch. |
| **2. Pain Recognition** | 2 | "ทำไมคนขยันถึงไม่ได้ดี? (คำตอบที่โรงเรียนไม่เคยสอน)" | Deepen the pain. Share a story. They're not alone. |
| **3. The Enemy** | 4 | "คนแบบนี้แหละ... ที่กำลังดูดเลือดคุณอยู่ในออฟฟิศ" | Name the enemy. Give them vocabulary for what they're experiencing. |
| **4. The Solution Tease** | 6 | "15 กฎที่ฉันอยากรู้ตั้งแต่ปีแรกที่ทำงาน" | Introduce the book naturally. Share one rule in full as proof of value. |
| **5. The Pitch** | 7 | "ประตูกำลังจะปิด — [ชื่อหนังสือ] พร้อมโบนัส" | Full sales email. Scarcity. Deadline. CTA. |

### Sequence 2: Post-Purchase Onboarding (7 emails, 14 days)

| Email | Day | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Download + Welcome** | 0 | Delivery. First win: "Read กฎข้อ 1 tonight. It takes 12 minutes." |
| **2. Quick Win** | 2 | "Try J.A.D.E. tomorrow. Here's exactly what to say." — Fast result builds trust. |
| **3. Deep Dive** | 4 | Direct reader to กฎข้อ 7-10 (People Radar). "This week, just WATCH." |
| **4. Testimonial Request** | 7 | "Has anything changed yet? Reply and tell me." — Social proof harvesting. |
| **5. Soft Upsell** | 9 | "Want to SEE these techniques demonstrated?" → Masterclass pitch. |
| **6. Bundle / Referral** | 11 | "Know someone else trapped in a toxic office? Forward them this." + affiliate tease. |
| **7. Community / Next Step** | 14 | "You've got the rules. Now stay sharp." → Newsletter signup, community invite. |

### Sequence 3: Abandoned Cart (3 emails, 3 days)

| Email | Timing | Subject |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Reminder** | 1 hour | "คุณลืมอะไรบางอย่าง..." (You forgot something...) |
| **2. Objection Handle** | 24 hours | " 'แพงไปไหม?' — ลองคิดแบบนี้ดูนะครับ" (Too expensive? Think about it this way.) |
| **3. Last Chance** | 72 hours | "โบนัสจะหายไปใน 24 ชั่วโมง" (Bonuses expire in 24 hours.) |

### Sequence 4: Long-Term Nurture (Weekly)

After the sequences complete, subscribers enter a weekly newsletter:
- One valuable tip or technique per week
- Occasional story about workplace survival
- Periodic re-promotion of the book + masterclass
- "Reader story of the week" — social proof engine

## 5. Key Marketing Angles (From Our Research Data)

### Angle 1: The "Game You Were Never Taught"
**Research anchor**: `bar_complaint_monologue` — "Nobody told me there was a WHOLE OTHER GAME happening right in front of me"
**How to use**: Every piece of copy should hammer home: you're not bad at your job. You were never taught the real rules. This book IS the rulebook.

### Angle 2: The "Self-Defense, Not a Weapon"
**Research anchor**: Objection #1 — "This sounds manipulative. I don't want to become like them."
**How to use**: Front-load every sales page with: "This is NOT a guide to manipulating people. This is a guide to making sure NOBODY can manipulate YOU." The Human Survival Guide already does this — the intro says exactly: "หนังสือเล่มนี้ ไม่ได้เขียนมาเพื่อสอนให้คุณเป็น 'คนเลว' แต่เขียนมาเพื่อเปลี่ยนคุณให้เป็น 'คนดีที่มีเขี้ยว'"

### Angle 3: The "They're Counting On You Never Learning This"
**Research anchor**: Story Section 6 (Evidence) — "The people currently outmaneuvering you already know all of this. They're counting on you never learning it."
**How to use**: This is the urgency engine. Every deadline, every CTA, every "order now" is backed by: the manipulators already know these techniques. Every day you don't learn them is a day they have the advantage.

### Angle 4: Proof-Rich Credibility
**Research anchor**: Proof elements — Harvard Business Review, 567-employee APA study, Machiavelli's 500-year track record
**How to use**: Even in Thai marketing, the English academic proof carries weight. Quote HBR. Quote the APA study. Show this isn't opinion — it's documented human behavior.
