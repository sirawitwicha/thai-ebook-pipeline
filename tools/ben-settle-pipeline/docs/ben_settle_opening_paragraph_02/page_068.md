“My name is Betty Adams and the first thing you should know
about me is I am not a doctor. I am not an expert on nutrition
either. I never went to college and I don’t have a degree in
anything. In fact the only thing I consider myself an expert about

And then it goes on to say I can show you how to lose twenty pounds
really quick.

But notice all the negatives and flaws, first.

There’s nothing here to show her as a credible person, but the one thing
she does say she’s an expert about is 100% believed because there’s all these
things that she’s not an expert about. There are so many ways you can go
about doing this, too. One opener I used extremely successfully is a variation
on what I saw Dan Kennedy do many years ago:

**Yes, this IS a “sales letter.”**

Brutal honestly.

So use your imagination to think up ways to be brutally honest in your
opening paragraph. Only a few ads ever do it, but the few ads that do tend to
do very well.
