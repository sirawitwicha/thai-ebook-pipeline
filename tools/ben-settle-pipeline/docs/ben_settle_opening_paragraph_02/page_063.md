Onwards to the second part of the 5-step Copy Slacker formula:

**The Opening Paragraph.**

This is the second most important part of your ad, in my opinion. Some people will say the PS is the second most important part. Especially in direct mail, and I have no doubt that was the case back in the day. But in my experience today — especially online — it’s this opening paragraph. I suppose some people will debate and split hairs about this if they want, quoting this guru and that guru.

And that’s fine if they want to waste their time.

But, I once heard the great John Carlton explain it like this:

The headline gets the prospect’s attention, and then the opening paragraph gets them situated, settled in, and...

**Ready And Willing**
**To Read Your Ad.**

In other words:

The headline flagged their attention and the opening paragraph bags that attention. Which is why if you get this opening paragraph right they’re set and they’re ready to absorb whatever information you say.
