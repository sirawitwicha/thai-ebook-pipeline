- **Reading level**: 10th-12th grade. Intelligent but accessible. Short paragraphs. Punchy sentences. No academic jargon.

- **Pace**: Urgent but not frantic. They feel the pain NOW but the solution takes time. Urgent problem + measured solution = credibility.
- **Relationship**: **The Strategist Who's Been There** — not the guru who has it all figured out, not the academic who only studied it. You've been in the trenches, you've been burned, you've figured out the patterns, and now you're handing over the playbook. Insider, not outsider. Battle-hardened, not naive.
