## Template Specs

| Element | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Length | ~4,000-5,500 chars (2-3 pages) |
| Sections | 3-5 per chapter |
| Data/numbers | At least 1 concrete calculation |
| Rhetorical Qs | 2-3 per chapter |
| "ครับ" usage | Every 3-5 sentences as rhythm |
| Curse words | 0-3 per chapter ("แม่ง", "โคตร" — NO มึง/กู) |
| Cliffhanger | Always end with hook for next chapter |

## Anti-Patterns (From Actual User Feedback)

❌ Pantip quotes as chapter hooks — "look too cheap"
❌ มึง / กู in ebook body — zero tolerance
❌ Chapters under 3,000 chars — too short, lacks depth
❌ Generic positive conclusions — "แล้วชีวิตคุณจะดีขึ้น"
❌ Superficial hot takes without data backup
